What is it?

Not a notary.
Legally required.

A Medallion Signature Guarantee certifies your identity and your legal right to transfer securities. The issuing institution carries a federal surety bond and assumes full legal liability — which is why most banks only issue them for their own customers.

We partner with STAMP2000-certified institutions to issue them online for everyone else.

01
Required by every major transfer agent

A standard notary is not a substitute. Computershare, EQ, AST, Broadridge — all require it.

02
Issued through STAMP2000 — the only authorized program

Every stamp we issue comes from a federally-certified program member. No shortcuts.

03
Done online in 24–48 hours

Identity verification, document prep, and official stamping — no bank visits.

When you need one

Required whenever securities
change legal ownership.

⚖️

Inheritance & Estates

Transferring stocks from a deceased parent or grandparent to heirs. Required regardless of value.

🎁

Gifting Securities

Moving shares between living individuals — parent to child, any gift — requires signature authentication.

🏛

Trust Transfers

Moving assets into or out of a trust, UTMA, or changing the trustee on record.

💍

Divorce & Name Changes

Court-ordered transfers and post-marriage name change account updates.

📜

Physical Certificates

Converting old paper stock certificates into a brokerage account.

Computershare → Brokerage

Moving DRS-held shares into Fidelity, Schwab, or Robinhood typically requires a Medallion.
