What is it?

Not a notary.
More powerful.

A Medallion Signature Guarantee is a federally-required certification that your identity and your legal right to transfer securities are authentic. The issuing institution carries a regulated surety bond and assumes full legal liability — which is why most banks only stamp for their own customers.

We partner with STAMP2000-certified institutions to issue them online for everyone else.

01
Required by every major transfer agent

A standard notary is not a substitute. Computershare, EQ, AST, Broadridge — all require it by federal regulation.

02
Issued only through STAMP2000

Every stamp we issue comes from a federally-certified program member. No shortcuts, no workarounds.

03
Done online in 24–48 hours

Identity verification, document preparation, and official stamping — entirely online.

When you need one

Required whenever securities
change legal ownership.

01

Inheritance & Estates

Stocks from a deceased parent or grandparent. Required regardless of estate value or brokerage.

02

Gifting Securities

Moving shares between living individuals requires authenticated signature and legal authority.

03

Trust Transfers

Into or out of a trust, UTMA, or changing the trustee on record.

04

Divorce & Name Changes

Court-ordered transfers and post-marriage name change account updates.

05

Physical Certificates

Converting old paper stock certificates into a brokerage account.

06

Computershare → Brokerage

Moving DRS-held shares into Fidelity, Schwab, or Robinhood.
